Here is how its done
When you hover your mouse over a suggestion, a set of tools pops up.
Use the stars panel to express your feelings regarding a suggestion
  • 5 Star

    Adds the video to the playlist and many more vidoes like it
  • 4 Star

    Pretty good, add it and a few more
  • 3 Stars

    Well... add just this one
  • 2 Star

    Kinda bad - remove from suggestions
  • 1 Star

    Sucks big time - remove this entire suggestion group

Q: Why do I see this message?

A: When you download a program from the Internet, SmartScreen Filter will check the program against a list of programs that are downloaded by a significant number of other Internet Explorer users and a list of programs that are known to be unsafe. If the program you're downloading isn't on either list, SmartScreen Filter will display a warning. It doesn't necessarily mean the website is fraudulent or that the program is malware.
The default behavior of SmartScreen does not allow many 3rd party applications to run when they’re downloaded
